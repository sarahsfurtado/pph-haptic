A.2 Haptic Interview with Dr Arun (NUH)

What does a healthy uterus feel like:

The same thing feels like a cricket ball under a yoga mat.

What does a soft and boggy uterus feel like:

It lacks the expected muscular resistance. Can be compared to a marshmallow, and not well toned.

How is uterine atony diagnosed:

The pressure is applied at the level of the umbilicus, which is the weakest part making it the easiest for the doctor to feel. If the uterus is normal, there will be an obvious stand out at the abdomen area. If not, it is located high and there is a struggle to find it. Consistent, firm, horizontal pressure will be applied for analysis.

Are students able to apply the correct amount of pressure to determine hardness?

They are not trained, this is a matter of experience. Not taught to students how much force to apply.
